# Blogger

The life goal of blogger is to help me achieve my goal to create a blog every week.

I currently have a blog, but I was never relly active, and now it has grown stale.

In the time when I started, until now, my technical expertise changed. I am currently working as an Enterprise Data Architect. My expertise is around data governance, data bricks and Microsoft Azure.

That's also the topices I like to blog about.

I want to create a blog that is special, a blog that is me.

## Audience

The first target adience is myself; writing posts, to help me remember, to helk me learn. I want to write about real problems I see or have.
I don't mind making mistakes or even being wrong.

For the rest, the audience is knowlegeable, I don't want to do too simple posts, unless it is for explaining things that I am just learning. How to make difficult problems easy.


## Expectations

### Tone of voice
Tone of voice is really important. I want it to be a little funny, but serious at the same time.
I don't mind making a fool of miself, to bring over the message.
