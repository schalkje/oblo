# Oblo

**Oblo** is an AI writing companion that helps transform ideas into clear, engaging, and well-structured blog posts.

The name is a coined word inspired by **blog**, while remaining short, memorable, and approachable. Rather than describing a specific feature, **Oblo** is intended to feel like the name of a trusted assistant—one that helps you brainstorm, organize your thoughts, improve your writing, and refine every draft until it's ready to publish.

### The idea behind the name

While **Oblo** is primarily a brand name, it also has a simple backstory:

- **O** = **Original**, **Organized**, **Optimized**
- **Blo** = **Blog**

Together, **Oblo** can be interpreted as **"Organize Blog"**—an assistant that helps turn original ideas into organized, optimized blog posts.

This meaning isn't meant to be literal, but rather a memorable way to capture Oblo's purpose: helping writers shape their thoughts into content worth publishing.

> **From idea to publishable blog—one conversation at a time.**
